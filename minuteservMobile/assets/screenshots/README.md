# Phone Screenshots for Google Play Store

## Required Screenshots (4 screenshots)

Upload these 4 screenshots to Google Play Console:

1. **screenshot-1-home.png** - Home screen (Beauty services at doorstep)
2. **screenshot-2-services.png** - Services screen (Service packages)
3. **screenshot-3-bookings.png** - Bookings screen (Upcoming bookings)
4. **screenshot-4-cart.png** - Shopping Cart screen

## Requirements

- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Size: Up to 8 MB each
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 9:16
- Dimensions: Each side between 320px and 3,840px
- Recommended: At least 1080px minimum for promotion

## How to Add Screenshots

1. Save your 4 screenshots to this folder
2. Name them: screenshot-1-home.png, screenshot-2-services.png, etc.
3. Upload them to Google Play Console → Store Listing → Phone Screenshots
