## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Rules

### Redemption Rules:
1. **Minimum Redemption:** 100 points (‚Çπ10 discount)
2. **Redemption Increment:** Must be multiple of 100 points
3. **Maximum Per Booking:** Can use up to 50% of booking amount in points
4. **Voucher Expiration:** Vouchers expire in 30 days
5. **Cannot Combine:** One redemption per booking

### Points Rules:
1. **Points Never Expire** (in Phase 1)
2. **Points Non-Transferable:** Cannot gift points to others
3. **Points Non-Refundable:** Cannot convert back to cash
4. **Points Earned After Service:** Only after service completion, not booking
